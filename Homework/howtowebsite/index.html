How to Make Rainbow Cupcakes!

Ingredients +

  • 1 stick of softened butter
  • 1 1/6 cups of sugar
  • 3 egg whites
  • 1/2 tablespoon vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups of flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cups milk
  • Pillsbury vanilla frosting (any color)

Instructions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

Step 2

Mix together egg whites, vanilla, flour, baking powder, salt, milk, butter and sugar.

Step 3

Divide batter evenly into 6 different bowls.

Step 4

Mix the food coloring in each bowl of batter. You should have one red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and purple bowl.

Step 5

Put one spoonful of each color into each cup of a nonstick muffin tray. Go in descending order of the rainbow.

Step 6

Bake for 18-20 minutes. Let cool for 20 minutes before frosting

Step 7

Frost the cupcakes as desired.
