My Trip to China

I visited China my senior year of highschool with a large group of my classmates. We first landed in Beijing, where we visited the popular night market, the Forbidden City, and the Great Wall. Next, we flew to the Gansu Province and saw the rainbow mountains, visisted a monestary, and rode cammels in the Gobi Desert. Finally, we ended our trip in Shanghai by taking a river boat tour and going to the City God Temple Garden.
